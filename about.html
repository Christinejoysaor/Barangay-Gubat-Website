About Us

Barangay Gubat is located at Gubat Lagonoy Camarines Sur.
Gubat is a Barangay in the municipality of Lagonoy in the provice of Camarines Sur.

History

Gubat was originally a big barrio of Bulusan, inhabited by a few Tagalogs, Visayans and Albayanons who travelled on foot to hunt wild animals. These people built their homes close to the shore and called their location Buri, which today is the barrio of Buenavista.

Populations

Barangay Gubat, located in the municipality of Lagonoy, Camarines Sur, is a small but historically vibrant community. According to the 2020 Census, Gubat recorded a total population of 1,089 individuals, representing about 1.92 percent of the entire population of Lagonoy. This figure shows a slight decline compared to the 2015 Census, where the barangay had 1,136 residents living in 203 households, with an average household size of approximately 5.6 members.

Looking back at historical census records, Gubat has experienced notable shifts in its population growth over the years. From a modest 656 residents in 1990, the barangay’s population more than doubled in just five years, reaching 1,648 in 1995. The upward trend continued with 1,709 residents in 2000 and 1,849 in 2007, reflecting a period of steady growth. However, after this peak, the numbers began to decline. By 2015, the population had dropped significantly to 1,136, and by 2020, it further decreased to 1,089, marking an annual growth rate of –0.89 percent during this period.

School

Gubat Community School is located at Barangay Gubat, Lagonoy, Camarines Sur. Four and one-half kilometers (4.5 km) from the poblacion. The school was established in 1966 during the Japanese regime.

Gubat Elementary School serves as the primary educational institution in Barangay Gubat. It provides quality basic education to children in the community, helping them develop knowledge, values, and skills for their future. The school also supports community programs, extracurricular activities, and initiatives that strengthen both learning and local development.
